Get your webhook
At the top right of the page, click on the User icon.
Your unique webhook url and Google key should appear, copy them to the Google Form Ad(s) that you wish to link to miniCRM.
Connect with Google Form Ads
  1. Your unique webhook url and Google key should appear, copy them to the Google Form Ad(s) that you wish to link to miniCRM.
  2. On the Google Ads site, this can be found while editing a form in the section "Lead Delivery Option" > "Manage your leads with a webhook
  3. Copy and paste your webhook url and Google key from miniCRM into the respective fields in your form.
  4. Click save at the bottom of the Form page.
 All set!
Congratulations! Your form is ready to delivery leads for you to view here! As soon as you receive a new lead, you will be able to view it on the "Leads" page!
